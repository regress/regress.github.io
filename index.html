ABOUT REGRESS

Regress dynamically rewrites live and pre-existing HLS streams to include targetted or broadcast ads. Ads are included seamlessly on any device, and cannot be removed by ad blocking software.

FEATURES

Live and VOD

Insert ads at any time into pre-existing or live content through our video CMS or live APIs.

Ad Network Compatability

With support for VAST and VPAID ad packages integration with almost any ad provider is easy.

Targetted Ads

Broadcast ads, or serve targetted ads on a per user basis. Even with live content.

Integration

Regress will work with any video player, but we provide libraries for every major platform to create the best user experience possible.

Analytics

Access detailed analytics to create reports on ad and content consumption.
